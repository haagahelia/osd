05.01 Sum of Two Numbers

Difficulty: easy
Size: S


Can you use JavaScript to add two numbers together and write the sum into the browser console?

Define the following three variables: x, y, z. Assign 3 to x. Assign 4 to y. After that, assign the sum of x and y to z. Finally, write the value of z into the browser console.
