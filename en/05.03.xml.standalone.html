05.03 Decimal Division

Difficulty: easy
Size: S


Now we try out how decimal division works. See how many decimals the system includes in the quotient. ('quotient' = the number that results when one number is divided by another).

Define the following two variables: a and b. Assign 4.87 to a and 5.03 to b. After that, write the quotient of a and b into the browser console.
