05.06 Unit Fine

Difficulty: medium
Size: S


A car driver (a motorist) was driving too fast. He drove 20 km/h over the speedlimit, so the police needed to fine him. Finnish fine units are based on the net income of the driver. One fine unit can be calculated as: (net income - 255)/60. Create a program that tells how much is one fine unit for inputed net income.

  • Define a function that calculates the fine unit (euros) based on given net income (euros).
  • Function call toFixed(2) returns the value of a number rounded to two decimals
