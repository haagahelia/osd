03.01 Log in to Linux and Mount Myy

Difficulty: easy
Size: S


First, open the page https://ondemand.cp.haaga-helia.fi and log in to the CentOS Linux environment.


(You can access the ondemand-system directly from Haaga-Helia campus network. If you are connecting from an external network, open first VDI desktop from http://vdi.haaga-helia.fi and from there connect the ondemand system.)


Double-click the icon "myy.sh" on your CentOS desktop and select "Run". Type your password into the opening window and check that the directory /mnt/myy contains now your files from the Myy-server.


Then take a screenshot of the Linux desktop and save the screenshot image to your public_html folder on the Myy server.


Finally, submit a link to the screenshot image.


(CentOS will be reseted every night, so don’t save anything important to the local hard drive of CentOS.)
